How scoring works

The system uses a trained neural network that learned from 2,400+ historical hiring decisions. It analyzes job requirements against candidate qualifications using natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning. The model extracts key skills, experience, and requirements from both texts, then predicts a match score from 1 (poor fit) to 10 (excellent fit) based on patterns learned from past successful and unsuccessful hires.
